# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **Coffee Shop sales data** using **Microsoft Excel** to uncover valuable business insights. An interactive dashboard was developed using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel formulas** to monitor sales performance, customer footfall, product demand, and purchasing trends.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions by visualizing key performance indicators (KPIs) and identifying sales patterns across different store locations, product categories, weekdays, and business hours.

---

## 🚀 Project Objectives

- Analyze total sales and customer footfall
- Identify peak business hours
- Compare sales across different store locations
- Analyze product category performance
- Identify top-selling products
- Understand customer ordering behavior
- Compare order distribution by drink size
- Monitor weekday sales trends
- Build an interactive dashboard using Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs

- 💰 Total Sales
- 👥 Total Footfall
- 💵 Average Bill per Person
- 🛒 Average Orders per Person

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Interactive Month and Day Slicers
- Quantity Ordered by Hour Analysis
- Category-wise Sales Distribution
- Drink Size Distribution
- Store-wise Sales & Footfall Comparison
- Top 5 Products Based on Sales
- Weekday Sales Analysis
- Dynamic Dashboard Filtering


## 📊 Key Insights

- Coffee is the highest revenue-generating product category.
- Morning hours experience the highest customer footfall and sales.
- Hell's Kitchen records the highest overall sales among all store locations.
- Barista Espresso is the top-selling product.
- Regular-sized drinks account for the largest share of customer orders.
- Sales remain relatively consistent across weekdays.
